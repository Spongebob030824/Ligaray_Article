Welcome to Cebu Technological University - Centrum

Cebu Technological University (CTU) is recognized as a leader in the field of education, research, and technology in the Philippines. One of the standout features of CTU’s various campuses is the impressive CTU Faculty Centrum. This modern facility embodies the university's commitment to creating an environment conducive to learning, innovation, and collaboration.

The Centrum is not just a building—it represents CTU’s drive toward excellence and continuous improvement in educational facilities. Located in a picturesque area of the campus, it offers both students and faculty a versatile space for various activities ranging from academic discussions, meetings, and social gatherings, to special events. This facility serves as a symbol of CTU’s dedication to fostering a holistic academic experience.

Facilities Inside the CTU Centrum

The interior of the Centrum is designed with both functionality and aesthetics in mind. Comfortable lounges and well-lit spaces greet students and visitors alike. The openness of the interior layout promotes collaboration and conversation, while the modern furniture provides a relaxing atmosphere for casual meetings or study sessions.

Leisure and Relaxation

One of the highlights of the Centrum is the pool area, where students and faculty can unwind and relax. The swimming pool is part of the university’s efforts to encourage a balanced lifestyle among its students. The surrounding area is also an ideal spot for outdoor gatherings, fostering a sense of community and engagement.
