About Cebu Technological University

Cebu Technological University (CTU) is a multi-campus public university system in the Philippines. With its central campus in Cebu City, it has multiple satellite campuses across the province, all striving for excellence in delivering education in fields like engineering, technology, and agriculture.

CTU Danao, which hosts the Faculty Centrum, is one of the prominent campuses that focus on both academic achievement and research. The Faculty Centrum serves as a hub for both academic and social functions within the university, providing students and faculty members a conducive environment to engage in both learning and extracurricular activities.
